# Privacy Policy for Feats

Last Updated: April 6, 2026

Feats does not collect, store, or share any personal information, usage data, or diagnostic information. 

1. Data Collection
We do not collect any information, personal or otherwise. The application functions entirely locally on your computer. All data you enter, including rides and routes, is stored exclusively on your local device and is never transmitted to any server.

2. Analytics/crash reporting
This application does not use any analytics, crash reporting, or telemetry services.

3. Third-Party Access
Since the application does not collect any information, no data is shared with third parties. 

4. Data Retention
We do not collect data, and therefore, no data is retained or stored on our servers. 

5. Email/Support
If you email the developer, your email address may be retained only to reply to your inquiry. This information is never used for marketing purposes. 

6. Consent
By using this application, you consent to this privacy policy.
